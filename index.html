TOP TEN ANIMES FOR FREE!!

Check Out the hottest top tens we picked for you from the Anime World.

Dragon Ball

Created by Akira Toriyama this is probably the most recognised and culturaly impacting anime of all time. This Anime follows the story of an extra terrastial being Goku who is of a race Called Saiyans. Their Goal is to destoy the population of the planet on which they land on. Due to some events Goku was unable to do this and now his story has become a legend to be told.

One Piece

Written by Ichiro Oda. It is a story about a boy who wants to become a pirate king and hence sets out on a adventourous journey and on his way makes new friends, gaining new powers, facing tremodous foes, and surpassing limits just to save his friends. Join in on this awesome journey of luffy to become a KAIZOKU OU!!

Death Note

Light Yagami a very bright student of Japan sees the world as a rotten place with people full of evils and criminals. The world really acknowledge his existence when a Shinigami gets bored from his realm and decides to venture out and drop a notebook into the human world. It just so happens that Yagami finds the note book and realises that this is note any notebook. Any person's name you write in it shall die within the 40 seconds, provided the writer must know his face and Name. L, world's best detective who is anonymous takes this case in his hands and soon this world becomes a chessboard with many pawns and pieces. Watch the heights to which both of them go to achieve victory over each other because no one is ready to accept Defeat. A mind-blowing battle between two mortals but one is no less than a god, Attarashi Sekkai No Kami, KIRA!!

Naruto

No matter what! HOKAGE NI NARU YO !! With these words a child Uzumaki Naruto, a ninja from the hidden leaf village Konoha faces insanely difficult challenges to fulfill his dream he once saw since his childhood. Each ninja is assigned a group and mentor. Kakashi Hatake the mentor of Uzumaki Naruto , Uchiha Sasuke and Someone not relevant goes on to perform a simple task assigned to the village ninja groups. Soon they realise that this small task would make them see the face of death for the first time and for the first time that death will see 'The Naruto'. Kishimoto Masashi created a rich past of all these Characters and I really advise to watch it once to know the truth of life.

Black Clover

In a world full of magic where evryone posses magical powers there exist a boy who can't harness the power of it and his desire is to become a wizard king. To accomplish this he trains himself and able to possess a magical book a help from another time.

Full Metal Alchemist

Two Brothers trying to revive their deceased mother through alchemy. Faces unforseen consequences because of the unbreakable law of alchemy. The Law of Equivalent Exchange. Edward Elric the elder brother and Alphonse Elric both of them loses parts of their body or rather the whole body. Follow them through their journey to get back the body of Alphonse. Learning more about this exotic Science of alchemy.

Attack on Titans

Shinzo wo Sasageyo!! Dedicate your Hearts like these soldiers of Survey Corps unit does to ellimante the greatest foes of humanity yet, The Titans. They look like humans but are gigantic in size some spanning a couple of meters more than human height and some Towering even the 50 meters wall beyond which humanity cannot exist. Eren Yeager makes this his goal to eliminate every single Titan from the face of the earth. Initially a revenge saga soon turns into an unthinkable pollitical ideological warfare. This masterpiece is a must watch to witness the creative heights a human brain can achieve. Cudos to Isayama Hajime.

Demon Slayer

Humans and demons exist in a world and demons cannot move in sunlight. Tanjiro Kamado's family gets devoured by a demon his sister Nezuko Kamado who ends up being a demon too, Unlike other demons she can control her urge to kill and eat humans.Tanjiro joins demon slayer corps to seek a cure for her sister. Meanwhile fighting demons and gaining more knowledge about these beings called 'demons'.

Seven Deadly Sins

Amidst the war between demons and angel a love story buds between Miliodas and Elizabeth. One being son of a Demon king and other Being a daughter of a God. Demon king puts a curse of immortality on Miliodas and Elizabeth is going to be reincarnated countless times with no memories of her previous life Miliodas. Miliodas and his 7 demon friends representing each deadly sin now seeks a way to break this curse of Elizabeth so the the love story could Prosper.

3000 years after earth was petrified due to a mysterious reason. Senku Ishigami a Doctor wakes from his peitrified state and he takes on the daunting task to turn the whole populationback to human form.
